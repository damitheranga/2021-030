Literature Survey

Due to the enhanced upliftment of IT industry, many higher educational institutes including those of state and non-state sectors are offering IT degree programs. Many students are enrolled to such programs. As per University Grants Commission(UGC) of Sri Lankaâ€™s statistics in 2019, 12510 undergraduates have enrolled in the state universities to follow IT related degree programs [1] and it was stated that 146,089 of employees exists in the IT industry in 2019 according to a survey conducted by Information and Communication Technology Agency(ICTA) [2].

More Details

Research Gap

Existing researches have proposed various productive solutions for career selection and progression prediction in a particular career path.Most of researches have given limited assistance to IT undergraduates to improve themselves for better career opportunities and to identify suitable career paths according their skills[8].In the meantime, for the prediction of attrition there exists a limited number of solutions along with suggestions of remedial actions for the IT organization. The aim of this study is to implement a comprehensive solution for providing IT undergraduates with guidance to improve their future careers, predict career progression of IT employees along with it's associated salary ranges and recommend professional practices.Also to provide guidance on taking remedial actions for the organization to retain the employees. The proposed system is a web-based responsive application using machine learning Models to provide the necessary predictions of career selection and progression. Overall, this solution could be used as a career mentor for an IT employee throughout his career life.

More Details

Research Problem

Currently, IT industry is gaining popularity among the prevailing industries by attracting young individuals and talents to cooperate in its activities [3]. These provide a gateway to a pool of opportunities for the IT employees to achieve a professional success since it consist of wide range of job titles. Few of the job titles include Software engineer, Quality Assurance Engineer and Business analysts [4], where each career path possesses a unique combination of skills. For example a software engineer must possess strong skills like knowledge on programming languages and concepts, problems solving, ability to work as a team and multitasking[5]. Upcoming generations desirability towards the higher studies in IT and other disciplines varies due to certain impacts, such as current trends and social influences from companions. This has affected the motivation of the students eventually causing them to inhibit their academic performance [6]. Furthermore, during their studentship they are not aware of what skills and attributes are to be developed in order to be a suitable candidate for a certain job role. In addition, they are not aware of what are the job roles they will be suitable for with the current level of expertise, skills and attributes.

More Details

Main Objectives

The main objective of this project is creating a responsive Web based Application using a REST API for assiting the IT Students and Employees with career guidance.

Specific Objectives

  1. Prediction of career suitability and initial salary of IT undergraduates and fresh graduates

  2. Prediction of career progression, its associated salary range progression in a respective career path and professional skill recommendation of IT employees

  3. Prediction of IT Employee attrition, retention, and Remedial Actions.

  4. Candidate Selection using Resume,Github and Linkdin Profile

More Details

Methodology

To obtain the above predictions, users have to enter their details through forms displayed on the website. The details given by the user on Front-End will be stored in the database. These new, unseen input data, fed into the machine learning classification models which are deployed with flask server on Heroku cloud application platform. Then these classifiers provide the most appropriate career path and initial salary range for the undergraduates and fresh graduates in IT field as predictions. By evaluating this prediction, a student can have a broad idea about his or her future career path. Furthermore, it provides predictions about the progression outline, salary outcomes and the professional recommendations to assist the IT employee to have a smooth and a successful carrier lifestyle. To accomplish these objectives Machine Learning Pipeline was used for each component.
